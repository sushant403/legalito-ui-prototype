The Partnership - Who we are


We are an award-winning property law firm based in London and Guildford. Over the last 10 years, we have helped thousands of people buy and sell their homes. Our unique combination of legal expertise and a friendly, modern approach to service has propelled us to become a dominant player in the London and home counties market. 

We offer legal services for:

- House sale and purchase

- Remortgages

- Transfer of equity

- Declarations of trust


The Partnership – Why use us

Each lawyer on our team has a dedicated assistant to ensure there is always someone in the office who knows your case.


Our Agent Portal offers progression updates 24 hours a day / 7 days a week


We provide weekly summary updates throughout the transaction ensuring the most relevant information is always available 


A dedicated team of Account Managers is available to answer any questions and run through the process before instruction.


Client Services administrators are on hand to help with onboarding and ensure the client's paperwork is in order.


We charge a flat rate fixed fee on all transactions
